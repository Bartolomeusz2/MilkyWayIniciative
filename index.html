Habitable Worlds Observatory

The Habitable Worlds Observatory (HWO) is NASA's upcoming mission aimed at searching for and characterizing potentially habitable planets beyond our solar system. Building on previous concepts, HWO will focus on identifying at least 25 habitable worlds, closely examining their atmospheres for chemical "biosignatures" such as oxygen and methane, which could indicate the presence of life. HWO will utilize advanced spectroscopy and direct imaging to study these planets, including those orbiting different types of stars, from sun-like stars to cooler dwarf stars. This mission promises unprecedented sensitivity and resolution, providing valuable insights into the evolution of cosmic structures and the potential for life beyond Earth.
